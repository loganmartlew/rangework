# App Store Info

## App name

Rangework: Practice Planner

## Short description

Plan structured golf practice with drills, reps, and exact ball counts.

## Full Description

Stop hitting balls aimlessly. Start practicing with purpose.

Most range sessions are a bucket of balls and no plan. Rangework changes that. Build your practice before you leave the house, then follow it shot for shot when you get there.

Build drills, not guesswork

Create Practice Units - individual drills with a name, an ordered list of instructions, and a ball count for each step. Add a mental focus cue, notes, and a default club so every drill knows exactly what it's asking of you. "50-yard pitch shots" with three landing zones at 10 balls each? That's one unit, ready to reuse forever.
Compose full sessions

Combine your units into Practice Sessions - repeatable templates for a complete day at the range. Pick your drills, set how many times each one repeats, and override the club or notes per slot when you need to. Rangework tallies the total ball count as you build, so you know exactly what you're signing up for before you go.
Made for the range, not the round

Rangework is a planning and practice tool - not a GPS, scorecard, or swing analyser. It does one thing well: helping serious golfers structure deliberate practice and actually stick to it.

Features:
• Practice Units with ordered instructions and per-step ball counts
• Focus cues, notes, and default clubs on every drill
• Practice Sessions that combine units with repeat counts
• Live running ball-count total while you build
• Duplicate, edit, and delete sessions and units freely
• A club bag you control - enable only the clubs you carry
• Light, dark, and system themes
• Yards/metres and mph/km·h/m·s unit options
• Phone and tablet layouts, with a two-column overview on tablets
• Secure Google Sign-In with your plans saved to the cloud

Your practice data is private to your account and stays in sync across launches.

Practice with purpose. Download Rangework and make every ball count.
